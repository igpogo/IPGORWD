Assigment

  • Design and apply your own color schemes for both of the media queries created in Lesson 4. The contrast ratio for the mobile viewport color scheme should be AAA-rated for all type, and the contrast ratio for desktop/laptop viewports should be AA for all type.
        • Desktop:
          article
            background: peachpuff
            text: green
          navbar:
            block:background: seagreen
            a:background: seagreen
            a: text: white
            a:hover:text: lightyellow
            li:hover:background: orangered
        Mobile:
          article
            background: white
            text: darkred
          navbar:
            block:background: white
            a:background: black
            a: text: white
            a:hover:text: yellow (it doesn't show)
            li:hover:background: honeydew
            li:hover: text: darkred
  • Choose and assign your own custom fonts for the <h1> style definition for both media queries created in Lesson 4.
      • I chosed Roboto Slab serif for the h1 tag
  • Test your responsive page in different viewports. And if you have the option to do so, test your page in a mobile device.
